Purpose

One of the main purposes of this journal is to simply iterate on ideas.

Keeping a written record of these thoughts makes it easier to build idea threads.

Furthermore, a journal can also help with organizing these idea threads, finding a way to present the ideas and information in a way that is easy to understand.
